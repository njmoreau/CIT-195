App Title: The TB Quest Game
Author: Nick Moreau
Date Created: 10/4/17
Description: You are a member of a village that is undergoing a great famine.
		A drought has swept the land, and the water from the nearby river has become polluted by an unknown force.
		Due to the dwindling population, the village Elder sends you, one of the few capable inhabitants remaining, on a quest far to the North to find the source of the river’s pollution and stop it before the village dies.
